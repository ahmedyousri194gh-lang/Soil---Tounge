In many countries, farmers lack the knowledge to use modern technologies like Arduino and its sensors. This technology is invaluable, especially in agriculture, and would significantly contribute to national economies.

Therefore, we designed a device that helps farmers determine soil properties such as acidity, temperature, moisture, and salinity. This ensures higher yields and reduced losses.

The device is placed in soil and displays the optimal crop on its screen based on sensor readings. It can be fully powered by solar energy to operate continuously.
