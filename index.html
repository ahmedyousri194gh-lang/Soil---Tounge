Smart Soil Analysis for Maximum Yield

النسخة العربية

In many countries, farmers lack the knowledge to use modern technologies like Arduino and its sensors. This technology is invaluable, especially in agricultura, and would significantly contribute to the economies of these countries.

Therefore, we have designed a device to help farmers determine soil properties such as acidity, temperature, moisture, and salinity — ensuring higher yields and reduced losses.

The device is placed in the soil and displays the optimal crop on its screen based on sensor readings — eliminating effort, time, or losses. It can also be powered entirely by solar energy for continuous operation.

تحليل ذكي للتربة لأعلى إنتاجية

يُوضع الجهاز في التربة ويعرض المحصول الأنسب مباشرة على شاشته، ويعمل بالطاقة الشمسية بالكامل.
