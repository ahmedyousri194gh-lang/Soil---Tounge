Project Overview

In many countries, farmers lack the knowledge to use modern technologies like Arduino and its sensors. This technology is invaluable, especially in agriculture, and would significantly contribute to the economies of these countries. Therefore, we have designed a device to help farmers determine soil properties such as acidity, temperature, moisture, and salinity. This helps ensure higher yields and reduced losses. The device is placed in the soil and displays the optimal crop on its screen based on sensor readings, eliminating any effort, time, or losses. Furthermore, the device can be powered by solar energy to ensure continuous operation.
